Faucet for FranklinCoin Blockchain

Welcome to the Faucet using FranklinCoin Blockchain. The FranklinCoin Blockhain is a Blockchain created by Franklin Perez as part of the Kingsland University Building Blockchain Project 2019 assignment.
  • This faucet sends up to 1 coin (i.e., 1,000,000 micro-coins) or less to anyone who requests coins.
  • The maxumum number of coins that you may request within any one-hour period is 1 coin (i.e., 1,000,000 micro-coins) or less.
  • Limits: one request per public address per hour.
  • Please go to the Faucet tab and enter the following to get your coins:
    • Your Public Address (40-Hex lowercase digits)
    • Amount of Micro-coins you would like to receive (maximum is 1,000,000 micro-coins)
    • Fee (in micro-coins) deducted from your amount to be given to the Miners - Minimium is 10 micro-coins.
    • Blockchain Node (URL Format) you would like to connect.
